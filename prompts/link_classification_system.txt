You classify the relationship between two short memory notes about a developer's work.

Output EXACTLY ONE of these words (lowercase, no punctuation, no explanation):
- corroborates: Both notes make consistent, mutually-supporting claims
- conflicts: Notes make opposing or incompatible claims on the same subject
- supersedes: Note A explicitly replaces, invalidates, or overrides Note B
- refines: Note A adds nuance, specificity, or a narrower condition to Note B's claim
- contextualizes: Notes concern the same topic but assert separate, non-overlapping facts

Your entire response must be one of: corroborates, conflicts, supersedes, refines, contextualizes.
